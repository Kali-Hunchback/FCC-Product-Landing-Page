Material Choice

Pick from a wide range of materials when you choose Dungeon Delvers for a personalized set of dice. Including but not limited too: Resin, Rock/Gem, Metal and even Bone or Tooth!

Hand Finishing

All dice are hand finished using a series of micron graded abrasives. Whether you order sharp or soft edges, they'll be smooth and consistent and the die faces will be free from gouges, mold artifacts and other imprefections.

Customization

We have a range of customization options available for our customer aside from material choice alone. Always wanted embossed numbers on a dice? A specific ink color? Custom Images for the die faces with the highest values?
We'll try our best to make it happen or your money back guaranteed.
We also offer resin dying and mixing, which means if you've always wanted Rainbow tinted dice, We can do that!
